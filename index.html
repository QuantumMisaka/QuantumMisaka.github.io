About

I work at the intersection of scientific discovery and engineering delivery in catalysis and materials simulation. My focus is on turning complex research problems into reproducible workflows and reusable open-source assets (from datasets and ML potentials to automation tooling).

Current interests: iron-based Fischer–Tropsch catalysis, surface reactions, active learning / fine-tuning for ML potentials, and community-facing tooling in the ABACUS / DeePMD ecosystem.
